About Me

I am a Technical Lead with 9+ years of experience designing and delivering enterprise-scale software across FinTech, Banking, Healthcare and Transit Payments. My core strength lies in architecting distributed, cloud-native platforms built on Java 21, Spring Boot, Microservices and Event-Driven Architecture.

I have led the end-to-end delivery of mission-critical payment and settlement systems — from contactless fare collection for public transit to Mastercard T-1 settlement and Visa Click to Pay integrations for global banks — with hands-on depth in CQRS, Saga, Kafka, ISO 8583 and OAuth2 security.

Beyond delivery, I focus on technical leadership: mentoring engineers, driving solution design and code quality, and championing modern, AI-assisted engineering practices across teams.
